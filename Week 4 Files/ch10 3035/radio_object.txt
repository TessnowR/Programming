	A RADIO OBJECT

What would be its ATTRIBUTES (data that describe its status)
- band, such as AM or FM
- station, such as 620 or 89.7
- volume, an integer from 1 to 10

What would be its METHODS (functions it performs)
- set_band, change band attribute to AM or FM
- set_station, select station attribute
- set_volume, raise or lower the volume attribute
- show_status, display all current attributes
